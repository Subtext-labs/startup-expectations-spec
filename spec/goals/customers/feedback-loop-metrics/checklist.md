# Checklist: Feedback loop and metrics

Tick when done. Adapt wording to your stage.

- [ ] Signals chosen match stage (usage, revenue, qual, support volume)
- [ ] Someone owns triage and a regular review cadence
- [ ] Feedback ties to backlog or experiment list
- [ ] Customers-facing comms when you act on feedback (when relevant)
- [ ] Privacy and consent match what you collect
