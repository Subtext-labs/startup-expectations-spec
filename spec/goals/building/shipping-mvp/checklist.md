# Checklist: Shipping your MVP

Tick when done. Adapt wording to your stage.

- [ ] Declared ship level (l0-internal through l3-public-mvp) and release criteria for that level only
- [ ] One primary job-to-be-done and hypothesis tied to this release
- [ ] Quality bar matches level: no silent harm; known gaps documented and accepted
- [ ] Learning plan: who reviews feedback, by when, and what decision follows
- [ ] Rollback or mitigation path exists for a bad release
- [ ] Stakeholders agree what signals count as success before you ship
