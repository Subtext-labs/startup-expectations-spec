# Checklist: Security and privacy posture

Tick when done. Adapt wording to your stage.

- [ ] Data you hold and why is documented (Security and privacy posture)
- [ ] Obligations for your market are identified
- [ ] Owner for reviews and incidents
- [ ] Vendors or subprocessors listed where required
- [ ] Customer-facing commitments match internal capability
- [ ] Change log when policy or posture shifts
