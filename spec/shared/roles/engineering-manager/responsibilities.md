# Engineering manager — shared expectations

Baseline for this role across industries. Company- or segment-specific files add detail on top.

## Leading people

- Clear goals, timely feedback, fair reviews.
- Hire and keep strong engineers; fix skill or capacity gaps with a plan.

## Shipping good work

- Balance roadmap and sustainable pace; say when tradeoffs are needed.
- Keep practices right-sized: reviews, tests, monitoring as fits your stage.

## Working across the company

- Show the team’s capacity and risks in planning.
- Partner well with product, design, and other engineering groups.
